Welcome to xGP!

xGP can assist general practitioners in their choice of specialist referral for a certain patient based on their symptoms and medical history. When pressing 'Start', you will be presented with a sequence of questions that can be answered by checking the right box regarding the patient. At the end of the questionnaire, the system will present a list of possible specialists a patient may need to go to. It saves time and reduces uncertainty in assessing the patient's condition.
